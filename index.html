🎯 All 8 Supported Gestures

👆

Move Cursor

Point with index finger only to control cursor smoothly across your screen

🤏

Left Click

Pinch thumb and index finger together (distance < 60px) to perform click

✌️

Scroll

Two fingers (index + middle) - move hand up for scroll up, down for scroll down

🖐️

Screenshot

Show all 5 fingers to instantly capture your screen

👍

Next Slide

Thumb only gesture - perfect for presentations (presses → key)

🤙

Previous Slide

Pinky only gesture - go back in presentations (presses ← key)

💍

Volume Control (FAST!)

Ring finger only - move hand LEFT (decrease 5%/frame) or RIGHT (increase 5%/frame) - SUPER FAST system volume control!

🖖

Brightness Control

Three fingers up - move left to dim, right to brighten your ACTUAL SCREEN
